FAQ

What is the point of this site?

This site is the final project for my web development class at Gwinnet Tech. However, I have a background in history, political science and I love the Humanities. So, I decided to build a site that would have a humanities aspect to it. At the same time, I am also a tutor for history so this site will be able to advertise my skills to others.

May one actually order tutoring services from the site?

At this current time, there is actually no real way to order tutoring services from the site. The e-commerce portion of this site does not actually allow you to place any real orders. It is all simulated.

Is the information on the site accurate?

All of the information found on this site is accurate and is backed up by a thorough bibliography-which one may look at the link for that page here. I guess you can say that in many ways I gave myself extra work ensuring that all of the information was accurate!

What will the books link show me?

The books page is recommendation of books that the user on the site may use to expand on the topics that this site presents.
